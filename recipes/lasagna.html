Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a cheesy layered pasta dish that people go cray-cray for.

Ingredients

  • Pasta squares with ragged edges
  • Cheeses, various, some amount
  • Tomato sauce?

Steps

  1. cook the pasta things (boil water etc according to the package)
  2. Lay down the pasta things in a pyrex dish covering it with one layer
  3. cover with a layer of tomato sauce
  4. cover with a layer of cheese
  5. repeat steps 2-3 until you reach the top of the dish or slightly under
  6. Bake at some heat in the oven (you should have preheated the oven as step zero. sorry bout that.)
