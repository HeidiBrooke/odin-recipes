Friendship

Description

Friendship is a relationship of a positive nature between two individuals. Generally enjoy laughing at similar things and try to understand each other. People go cray-cray for it.

Ingredients

  • person one
  • person two
  • sense of humor
  • care
  • gentle roasting
  • honest communication

Steps

  1. Observe other individuals
  2. approach the one the please you and make a joke
  3. if they laugh then ask,"Do you want to be friends?"
  4. if they say yes, fist bump and follow them around, if no proceed to final step.
  5. talk to them now and then or keep telling jokes
  6. when you leave their presence, say "Keep it goucci pal. See you tomorrow!"
