Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese salad is a minimal salad consisting of only tomato, basil and mozarella cheese. People go cray-cray for it.

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomoatoes (one container or to your liking)
  • Mozarella cheeseballs (or slice a cylinder of it.)
  • Basil leaves (many several)
  • Balsamic Vinegar (one smathering)
  • Olive oil (one smathering smaller than the vinegar
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Slice your cherry tomatoes in half (or not)
  2. Slice your basil leaves in half (or not)
  3. combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix
  4. OR present nicely on a plate as shown in illustration
  5. Nom dat shi-shi
